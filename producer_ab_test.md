# MINOR_SHIFT A/B Test Protocol

## Objective

Compare listener and producer response to MINOR_SHIFT vs. non-MINOR_SHIFT blueprints.

## Instructions

1. Provide producers with two sets of song blueprints (MINOR_SHIFT and control).
2. Have each producer create short demos for each blueprint.
3. Collect listener feedback via survey or streaming stats.
4. Gather producer notes on process and perceived hit potential.
