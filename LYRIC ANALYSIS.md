**Lyrical Pattern:** Repetitive affirmation + social commentary

**Theme:** Physical attraction, nightlife  
**Tone:** Sensual, direct, narrative

**Lyrical Pattern:** Body description + club scenario  
**Key Lines:** "I'm in love with the shape of you / The club isn't the best place to find a lover"

### 2016: "Love Yourself" - Justin Bieber

**Theme:** Post-breakup dismissal  
**Tone:** Sarcastic, dismissive, confident  
**Genre:** Acoustic pop  
**Lyrical Pattern:** Backhanded compliments + empowerment  
**Key Lines:** "My mama don't like you and she likes everyone / You should go and love yourself"

### 2015: "Uptown Funk" - Mark Ronson ft. Bruno Mars

**Theme:** Confidence, style, celebration  
**Tone:** Boastful, energetic, funky  
**Genre:** Funk revival + pop  
**Lyrical Pattern:** Style brags + dance commands  
**Key Lines:** "Don't believe me just watch / This one for them hood girls"

### 2014: "Happy" - Pharrell Williams

**Theme:** Pure joy, positivity  
**Tone:** Euphoric, simple, repetitive  
**Genre:** Neo-soul + pop  
**Lyrical Pattern:** Simple affirmation + emotional state  
**Key Lines:** "Because I'm happy / Clap along if you feel like happiness is the truth"
